About Vysya Sangamam

Vysya Sangamam Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2010 with the aim of preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of the Vysya community while engaging in social welfare activities that benefit society at large.

Our foundation brings together like-minded individuals committed to cultural preservation, community service, education, and economic empowerment.

Our Mission & Vision

Cultural Preservation

Document and promote Vysya traditions, art forms, language, and cultural practices through events, workshops, and digital archives.

Educational Support

Provide scholarships, career guidance, and skill development programs for students from economically weaker sections.

Community Service

Organize health camps, charity drives, and welfare programs benefiting all sections of society without discrimination.

Our Key Activities

1

Annual Cultural Festival

A grand celebration featuring traditional music, dance, crafts, and cuisine showcasing Vysya heritage.

2

Youth Empowerment Programs

Career counseling, mentorship, and entrepreneurship workshops for young community members.

3

Elderly Care Initiative

Monthly health check-ups, social gatherings, and financial support for senior citizens.

4

Digital Library Project

Preserving rare manuscripts, folklore, and historical documents in digital format.

5

Business Networking

Regular meet-ups and conferences to foster business connections and economic growth.

6

Charity Drives

Food distribution, winter clothing drives, and educational kit distribution for underprivileged children.
